Mission

We truly care about each client, and it comes across. Our mission is to not only get a better result for clients, but to make the process less stressful. That means being more transparent about pricing and what to expect. It also means making ourselves available whenever you need us and walking you through each step of the way. And because we only take clients we know we can help, our team of tax experts can work harder on each case. You’re never just a number to us.

Meet Your Tax Relief Team

Scott L. Gettis, Enrolled Agent (EA), MSc, CEO

In review after review, Scott’s clients rave about his phenomenal service and their outstanding tax debt relief results.

Gene P. Haag, Enrolled Agent (EA), M. Ed, Managing Partner

Gene has over 10,000 tax consultations under his belt — and still gets excited about helping clients with tax relief. “Ultimately, it’s about being honorable and doing an honorable day’s work. Every single staff member comes in to work and does a great job for our clients. We are all a part of something bigger than ourselves.”

Michele Mulkey, CPA, SPHR, SHRM-SCP, Chief Operating Officer

Michele has led Precision’s people strategy, the key to our growth, since joining the team in 2018. As Director of Human Resources, she initiated multiple programs that fortified Precision’s “Best Places to Work” and “BBB Torch” awards, while growing the professional team from 23 to 60. In 2021, Michele accepted the role of COO, responsible for firm growth and strategic initiatives. We are excited to see how Precision will grow under her “people first” style of leadership.

Ryan Colón, Esq., Attorney, Licensed to practice before the US Tax Court

Ryan, an accomplished tax attorney, brings extensive expertise in tax representation to the table. His track record includes helping clients navigate intricate tax laws and resolving IRS disputes. He has a remarkable ability to harmonize robust analytical skills with a compassionate and empathetic approach. His clients consistently shower him with praise for his unwavering dedication and exceptional professionalism.

Aurae Hawkins, Enrolled Agent (EA), Manager of Client Services Team

Aurae is our Lead Financial Analyst, supervising the Case Coordinators and managing a caseload for our Senior Resolution Management team. Never one to stop learning, Aurae is pursuing her fellowship with the National Tax Practice Institute, a stringent three-level program covering all facets of representing taxpayers before the IRS.

Ariel Scott, Enrolled Agent (EA), MBA, Director of Fulfillment

Ariel oversees the resolution of our client’s tax cases from beginning to end. She explains, “People who are struggling with IRS compliance and debt trust us to help them navigate the IRS channels and negotiate on their behalf, but we also coach and guide them so that they are set up for future financial success.” Ariel loves to work out, stay active outdoors, play games with her two teenage sons, and read for her book club.

Melissa Miller, Enrolled Agent (EA), Senior Manager, Case Resolution

Melissa loves helping clients across the country. “It’s a wonderful feeling, letting a client know their tax debt is resolved.” Melissa takes advantage of the beautiful outdoors here in Idaho, hiking and camping with her family.
